2028 presidential odds

Probabilities for the 2028 race.

A static, shareable dashboard of Polymarket yes-price probabilities for the Democratic primary, Republican primary, and final presidential election. Probabilities are market-implied snapshots, not polling or endorsements.

Source: PolymarketActive candidate markets only
Notes: Prices are pulled from Polymarket's public Gamma API and rendered as percent probabilities from each binary “Yes” market. These independent candidate markets can add to more or less than 100% because of spreads, liquidity, fees, and long-shot pricing. Page deployed as a snapshot; source event pages are linked from each section.
